ACME Corp Employee Handbook - Benefits and Policies

VACATION POLICY:
All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation days must be approved by your direct manager at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.
Part-time employees receive vacation days on a pro-rated basis.

REMOTE WORK POLICY:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
All remote workers must be available during core hours of 10am to 3pm EST.
A stable internet connection is required for remote work.
Remote work privileges may be revoked if performance standards are not met.

HEALTH INSURANCE:
The company provides health insurance through BlueCross BlueShield.
Employee premiums are covered 80% by the company.
Dental and vision coverage is optional and costs $45 per month.
New employees are eligible for health insurance after a 30-day waiting period.

RETIREMENT BENEFITS:
The company offers a 401(k) plan with a 4% employer match.
Employees are eligible to enroll after 90 days of employment.
Vesting schedule is 25% per year over 4 years.

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT:
Business travel expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submission.
Meals during business travel are capped at $75 per day.
All expenses over $50 require a receipt.
Personal expenses are not eligible for reimbursement.
